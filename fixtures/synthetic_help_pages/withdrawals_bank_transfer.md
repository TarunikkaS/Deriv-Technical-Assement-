# Bank transfer withdrawals

## Minimum and maximum withdrawal amounts
The minimum withdrawal amount for bank transfer is USD 10 (or equivalent in your account currency). The maximum withdrawal amount per transaction is USD 10,000. There is no monthly cap, but very large withdrawals may be split into multiple transactions for compliance purposes.

## Processing time
Bank transfer withdrawals are typically processed within 1 to 3 business days on the Deriv side. After Deriv has approved and sent the withdrawal, your bank may take an additional 2 to 5 business days to credit the funds to your account, depending on your country and your bank's processing speed. International wire transfers may take longer.

## Fees
Deriv does not charge a fee for bank transfer withdrawals. However, intermediary banks or your receiving bank may charge a fee that is deducted from the transferred amount. For SWIFT transfers, the typical intermediary fee ranges between USD 15 and USD 35.

## How to request a bank transfer withdrawal
Sign in to your Deriv account, go to the Cashier, choose Withdraw, and select Bank transfer. Enter the amount, your bank account details (IBAN or account number, SWIFT/BIC code, bank name and address), and confirm. You may be asked to verify your identity before the first withdrawal of a given method.

## Currency conversion
If your bank account currency differs from your Deriv account currency, the conversion is performed by your bank at their prevailing exchange rate. Deriv displays the requested amount in your account currency only.
