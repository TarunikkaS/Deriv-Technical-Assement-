# Multiple Deriv accounts

## How many real accounts can I have
You can hold more than one real account on Deriv. In fact, most clients have several: a Deriv real account holds your fiat balance and is used for funding, while separate trading accounts (Deriv MT5, Deriv X, and Deriv cTrader) are used to trade specific markets.

You can also open additional Deriv real accounts in different fiat currencies (for example one in USD and one in EUR) and one cryptocurrency account per supported coin. This lets you keep balances in the currencies you trade with.

## Account types
- Deriv real account: holds funds and is used to fund all trading platforms.
- Deriv MT5: trades CFDs on forex, stocks, indices, commodities, cryptocurrencies, and Deriv synthetic indices.
- Deriv X: trades CFDs on the same asset classes through Deriv's own platform.
- Deriv cTrader: trades CFDs through the cTrader platform.

## How to open additional accounts
Go to Account settings, choose Add account, and select the account type and currency you want. Some account types may require additional verification depending on your country of residence and the regulator that oversees that account.

## Limits
Deriv does not permit duplicate accounts of the same type and currency for a single client. If you try to open a second account of the same type and currency, the request will be declined. If you need to change the currency of an existing account, contact support — currency changes are only allowed before the account has been funded.

## Managing multiple accounts
You can switch between accounts using the account selector in the top right of the Deriv interface. Internal transfers between your own accounts are typically instant and have no fee, except for currency conversion which is applied at the prevailing market rate.
