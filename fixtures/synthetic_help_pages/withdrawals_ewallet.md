# E-wallet withdrawals

## Supported e-wallets
Deriv supports a range of e-wallets for withdrawals, including Skrill, Neteller, WebMoney, Perfect Money, Sticpay, and AstroPay. Availability depends on your country of residence.

## Processing time
E-wallet withdrawals are typically the fastest method on Deriv. Most e-wallet withdrawals are processed within 24 hours by Deriv. After Deriv approves the request, the funds usually arrive in your e-wallet account within minutes — often instantly. In total, you should expect an e-wallet withdrawal to complete within 1 business day from the time you submit the request.

## Minimum and maximum amounts
The minimum e-wallet withdrawal amount is USD 5. The maximum per transaction varies by provider, typically between USD 5,000 and USD 10,000.

## Fees
Deriv does not charge a fee for e-wallet withdrawals. The e-wallet provider may charge their own fee — please check your e-wallet's terms.

## Requirements
Your e-wallet account must be in your own name. Withdrawals can only be sent to an e-wallet that has previously been used to deposit funds into your Deriv account, or to an e-wallet that you have separately verified with Deriv. This is a regulatory requirement to prevent fraud.

## Troubleshooting delayed withdrawals
If your e-wallet withdrawal has not arrived after 24 hours, check your Deriv withdrawal history. If the status is "Processed", contact your e-wallet provider with the reference number. If the status is "In review", Deriv compliance may need to verify your identity — they will reach you by email within 1 to 2 business days.
