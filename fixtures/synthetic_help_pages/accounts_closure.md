# Closing your Deriv account

## How to close your account
You can close your Deriv account by contacting customer support through Live Chat or by emailing support. State that you wish to close your account and confirm your identity using the registered email address.

## What happens to your open positions
When you request to close your Deriv account, your open positions are not automatically closed by Deriv. You must close all open trades on every linked trading platform — Deriv MT5, Deriv X, Deriv cTrader, and any options or multipliers contracts on Deriv.com — before account closure can complete.

If you submit a closure request while positions are still open, support will respond asking you to close the positions first. The account closure request will be paused until you do. Deriv staff will not close trades on your behalf, and the platform will not force-close them; this avoids any disputes about exit prices.

## What happens to your funds
Before the account is closed, you must withdraw any remaining balance from your Deriv account using a supported withdrawal method. Any unclaimed balance below the minimum withdrawal amount can be donated to charity (Deriv has a partnership with Brick by Brick) or, in limited cases, refunded by support.

## Reopening a closed account
A closed account can sometimes be reopened by contacting support, provided the closure was not for compliance reasons. If the closure was triggered by a compliance review, the decision is final and a new account cannot be opened.

## Data retention
Even after closure, Deriv retains your account records for a period required by financial regulations (typically 5 to 7 years depending on jurisdiction). This is for audit and anti-money-laundering compliance, not for marketing.
