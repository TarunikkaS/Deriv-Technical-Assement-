# Trading on Deriv — overview

## Markets and instruments
Deriv offers trading on forex, stocks and stock indices, commodities, cryptocurrencies, and Deriv-only synthetic indices that are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The exact instrument list depends on the regulator that oversees your account.

## Platforms
Trading is available on three main platforms:
- Deriv MT5 (MetaTrader 5)
- Deriv X (Deriv's own platform)
- Deriv cTrader

In addition, Deriv.com offers options and multipliers products through a web interface that does not require any installation.

## Account currencies
Trading accounts can be denominated in fiat currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, etc.) or in cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, USDT, LTC, BCH). The currency is selected when the account is created and cannot normally be changed afterwards. Funding a trading account from a Deriv real account in a different currency triggers a conversion at the prevailing market rate.

## Margin and leverage
Leverage on Deriv MT5 depends on the asset class and the regulator that oversees your account. Typical maximums are 1:1000 on forex majors, 1:300 on stock indices and commodities, and 1:30 on cryptocurrencies. Lower leverage applies for accounts under EU regulators. The exact leverage for each asset is shown on the contract details page.

## Spreads and commissions
Most Deriv MT5 accounts use spread-only pricing with no separate commission. Some account types (for example the "Zero spread" account) use a tighter spread plus a commission per lot. The applicable spread and commission for each instrument are shown on the platform.

## Risk warning
Trading CFDs involves significant risk and may not be suitable for all investors. You can lose more than your initial deposit. Always trade with money you can afford to lose, and consider using the self-imposed limits tools described in the Responsible trading help page.
