# Account verification for clients in Malaysia

## Why verification is required
Account verification (also called Know-Your-Customer or KYC) is required to comply with anti-money-laundering regulations and to keep your funds safe. Verification must usually be completed before your first withdrawal, though you can deposit and trade smaller amounts before verifying.

## Documents required for clients in Malaysia
Clients with a Malaysian address must submit:

1. Proof of identity — a clear scan or photo of one of:
   - Malaysian national identity card (MyKad), front and back
   - Valid Malaysian passport (photo page)
   - Malaysian driving licence

2. Proof of address — a document, less than 3 months old, showing your full name and current Malaysian residential address. Acceptable documents include:
   - Utility bill (electricity, water, gas, telephone, or internet)
   - Bank statement
   - Tax assessment notice from LHDN
   - Government correspondence

3. Selfie verification — a clear photo of you holding your identity document, used to confirm that the document belongs to you.

## What is not accepted
Do not submit screenshots of mobile-banking apps, edited or marked-up scans, expired documents, or documents in someone else's name. Documents must be in colour and the entire page must be visible.

## How to upload documents
Go to Account settings, choose Verification, and upload each document under the corresponding category. Most submissions are reviewed within 1 to 2 business days. You will receive an email when verification completes or if additional information is needed.

## After verification
Once your documents are approved, your account is fully verified. You can then withdraw funds without restrictions and access higher trading limits. You only need to verify once unless your documents expire or your address changes — in which case Deriv may ask for an updated document.
