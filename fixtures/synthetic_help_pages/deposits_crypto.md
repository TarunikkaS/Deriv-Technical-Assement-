# Cryptocurrency deposits

## Supported cryptocurrencies
Deriv supports deposits in Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT — ERC-20 and TRC-20), Litecoin (LTC), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), and several other major cryptocurrencies. The exact list available to you depends on your country of residence.

## Are there fees for crypto deposits?
Deriv does not charge a deposit fee for cryptocurrency deposits. However, you will pay the standard blockchain network fee (the "gas" fee or miner fee) to the network when you broadcast the transaction from your wallet. This network fee is paid to the blockchain, not to Deriv, and varies with network congestion.

## Minimum deposit amount
The minimum cryptocurrency deposit amount varies by coin. As examples: BTC 0.0002, ETH 0.005, USDT 5, LTC 0.05. Below these minimums, the deposit will not be credited. Always check the current minimum on the Cashier page before sending.

## Processing time
Crypto deposits are credited after a fixed number of network confirmations. For BTC this is typically 1 confirmation (about 10 to 30 minutes). For ETH and ERC-20 tokens, this is typically 12 confirmations (about 3 minutes). For TRC-20 USDT, this is typically 19 confirmations (under 1 minute). Once the required confirmations are reached, the deposit is credited automatically.

## How to deposit
Sign in, go to the Cashier, select Deposit, choose your cryptocurrency, and copy the deposit address (and memo/tag where applicable). Send the funds from your own wallet to that address. Do not deposit from an exchange or a wallet that is not in your own name — Deriv may freeze or return the deposit for compliance review.

## Conversion to fiat
Crypto deposits land in a Deriv crypto account in the same coin. To trade fiat-denominated CFDs you can transfer between your crypto and fiat Deriv accounts; the conversion rate is shown before you confirm.
